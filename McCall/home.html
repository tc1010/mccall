Welcome to Southwest Idaho

Welcome to Canyon County, Idaho! You will find endless recreation and entertainment options here in this outdoor adventurer’s paradise, from natural hot springs to skiing to off-roading to cave exploring. We have one of the best snowmobile trail systems in the world, and we are also the home of the one of the largest herds of wintering elk in the US. Many people come here to fish for Trout, Catfish, Bass, Pike and other species at beautiful Lake Lowell and on the Canyon River, and hunting is another favorite local pastime. We have fun indoor options as well, like the Whittenberger Planetarium and the Orma J. Smith Museum of Natural History, along with spas, boutiques, art galleries, and more, and our wine industry has shown tremendous growth over the last 50 years as well. Today we have over 1,200 acres of vineyards and nationally acclaimed wineries where you can attend tastings and go on tours. From theaters to nightlife to boutique shopping, there is something for everyone here, and you will never run out of ways to stay busy here.

Canyon County is one of the two strongest real estate markets in the state, and the median home price here is lower than the state average. There are over 350 houses for sale here today, starting at just $60K and going as high as just under $800K for private and serene acreage property with breathtaking views that has an outdoor area that was designed for entertaining with barbecue and seating areas, a fire pit, a covered patio, and more. One of the best values available in real estate today is new construction, which starts at just $100K. These stunning residences have everything today’s discerning buyer is looking for, from built-in reading alcoves with shelves and seating, wet bars with wine refrigerators, soaring high ceilings, gorgeous wood floors, chef’s island kitchens, stainless steel appliances, dramatic staircases, formal living and dining spaces, and spa bathrooms with Jack and Jill vanities, oversized tubs, and separate tiled showers. Another option here is farms and equestrian properties, so if you are a horse lover you will find something you’ll love here. To learn more about the local market and the services I offer, please get in touch with me today.
